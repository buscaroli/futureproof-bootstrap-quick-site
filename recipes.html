Pizza Marinara

One of the Two original Neapolitan Pizzas. Marinara's Pizza only has tomato sauce, oregano, basil and garlic with no mozzarella.

Pizza Margherita

The second of the two original Neapolitan Pizzas, Margherita is a classic and the most loved Pizza in Italy (and the World!): tomato and mozzarella.

Pizza Boscaiola

Another great choice is this red pizza with the twist of having a topping of mushrooms (champignon or porcini) and sausage (Boscaiola means "from the Woods").

Pizza Contadina

Can be the same as the Boscaiola but has a larger variety of vegetables and might not have any sausage topping.

Pizza Quattro Stagioni

A pizza made up by four wedges having different toppings, the best one to share with friends with different tastes!

Pizza Capricciosa

A traditional pizza with tomato sauce, mozzarella, artichokes, black olives and Prosciutto Cotto (cooked ham).
